## How to Use These Routes

### Direct Access
Visit any route directly in your browser:
```
https://your-domain.com/questions/graphs/graphs-1
```

### Sharing
Share the complete URL with team members or friends:
```
Hey! Check out this DSA problem: https://your-domain.com/questions/graphs/graphs-1
```

### Opening in New Tab

- The question opens in a new tab with its unique URL
